Internsavek

Year: 2025 Tech: FastAPI, Python, Eleven Labs API

The Challenge

The world of product management internships is vast and often confusing for students. Many either don't know where to look or end up in roles that don't match their skills or career goals. The challenge was to create a centralized, intelligent platform to solve this problem by simplifying the discovery and application process.

AI-Powered Solution

We developed 'Internsavek', an AI voice assistant that serves as a personal career counselor. By asking a series of targeted questions about a student's background, technical skills, and career aspirations, the assistant gathers key information to build a comprehensive user profile.

Personalized Matching

Our custom-trained machine learning model processes the user's answers and cross-references them with a continuously updated database of current PM internships. It then returns the top 10 most suitable matches, each including a percentage score and a clear ranking. This makes it easy for students to identify their best options and apply with confidence, streamlining their journey into the world of product management.

Score App

Year: 2025 Tech: React, Node.js, PostgreSQL

The Challenge

Judging large-scale events like hackathons or competitions is complex. It involves multiple judges, teams, rounds, and weighted scoring categories. Manually calculating and normalizing scores is time-consuming and prone to error, and judge bias can unfairly influence results.

A Centralized Solution

This Score App provides a comprehensive, real-time platform for event management. Admins can create events, define custom scoring categories with specific weights, and manage teams and judges. Judges get a clean, simple interface to enter scores, ensuring a smooth and efficient process from start to finish.

Advanced Fair-Scoring

The app's core feature is its advanced normalization engine. It uses Z-score standardization to correct for individual judge leniency or strictness, ensuring every team is evaluated on a level playing field. The system aggregates these normalized scores, calculates percentiles, and generates a final, unbiased ranking, with options for manual admin overrides and detailed audit logs.

MediChain

Year: 2025 Tech: Blockchain, Node.js, React, ZKP

The Problem

Traditional healthcare systems keep medical records in isolated silos, giving patients little to no control over their own data. Sharing records between providers is cumbersome and insecure, and patients have no transparency into who is accessing their sensitive information.

A Decentralized Solution

MediChain empowers patients by giving them complete ownership of their medical records through a decentralized platform. Using blockchain for consent management and IPFS for encrypted, off-chain storage, patients can grant or revoke access to their data in real-time. Every action is recorded on an immutable ledger, providing a transparent audit trail.

Advanced Privacy & Access

The platform leverages Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) to allow for data verification without exposing the raw medical records. For instance, an insurer can verify a claim's eligibility without ever seeing the full patient history. Additionally, a secure QR-code system grants temporary, limited access to first responders in emergency situations, ensuring critical information like blood type and allergies is available when it matters most.
